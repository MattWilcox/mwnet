This site is a playground - somewhere I can toy around without pressure.

I'm building it from scratch, without any plan or design, using it as an excuse to learn Eleventy in the process. There's not much here yet.

In my free time I love to read, write, and shoot amateur photography. I play with the occasional Arduino & Raspberry Pi project, and get a kick out of traditional hand-tool woodwork.

I'm lead developer at View Creative Agency. My job is to help make beautiful websites that perform well for both our clients and their audience.

My role includes project assessment and planning; system and server management; building HTML, CSS, and JS powered front-ends; CMS integrations; and helping develop our team into a force to be reckoned with. It's rewarding and fun stuff.

I've been a full-time web developer since 2004, and a keen hobbyist before that. I've been a member of the HTML Working Group and Responsive Images Community Group.
